Donauinselfest

The Donauinselfest is a free open-air music festival which occurs annually at Donauinsel in Vienna, Austria. The festival is hosted by SPÖ Wien. It is the largest open-air music festival in the world, with around 3 million visitors over 3 days.

Danube - magnificent river

In Vienna, the Danube is omnipresent, whether in the form of the Donau-Auen National Park or Old Danube, Danube Island or Danube Canal. This also means that the river banks and beaches are close by. The Viennese use it for recreation, games and sports or for pleasurable hours in the beach cafés, bars and restaurants on the Old Danube, as well as Copa Beach and Sunken City on Danube Island.
